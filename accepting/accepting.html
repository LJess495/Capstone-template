Accepting

Life will never be perfect. There is tragedy, insecurity, jealousy, conflict, but we must learn to be ok with that. That is accepting.

  • Next

Accepting demonstrates the process of being okay. From the aspects of ourselves and the contradictions of personality, to embracing past mistakes and the nothingness of life, while also exploring the stigmatism of not being allowed to express your feelings especially in the role society deems acceptable.

Works from: Francis, Amanda, Allison, Xinyao, Molonie, Joel

The Bench

Francis Alicka

Project Statement

You see the Spurs game last night? What do you want to do with your life? You trying to go to the gym? Ever think about the future? Some of these questions are not what you would normally hear in a conversation between guys. A lot of men will talk about the gym, sports, games, etc. Rarely ever do they talk about how they feel or anything related to that. I find it sad that at a young age, men are conditioned to not speak about how theyâ€™re feeling and go about their lives thinking that way. My friend Phillip is someone that I can have any kind of conversation with, and for that, I am thankful. The Bench is meant to be an insight into the kinds of conversations that my friend and I have.
